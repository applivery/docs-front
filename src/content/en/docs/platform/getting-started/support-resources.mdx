# What support channels are available in Applivery?

Applivery provides multiple support channels to help you succeed with device management and app distribution. Your available support options depend on your subscription plan.


**To access support:**

1. Click the **Tickets icon (üéüÔ∏è)** in the top bar next to your Workspace details
2. View your open tickets and their status
3. Click **Create New Ticket** to submit a support request
4. For live chat, click the **chat bubble** in the bottom-right corner of the Dashboard

You can track all your support tickets directly from the Dashboard, making it easy to follow up on platform issues or feature requests.

## Where can I find documentation and learning resources?

Applivery maintains comprehensive documentation and learning materials to help you master the platform.

**Official documentation resources:**

- **Learn Hub** - [learn.applivery.com](https://learn.applivery.com) - Complete documentation for Device Management and App Distribution
- **API Documentation** - [applivery.com/docs/api](https://www.applivery.com/docs/api) - REST API reference, endpoints, and integration guides
- **Blog & Guides** - [applivery.com/blog](https://www.applivery.com/blog) - Best practices, industry insights, and product updates

**Key documentation sections:**

- **Getting Started** - Initial setup, workspace creation, and first device enrollment
- **Device Management** - MDM/UEM guides for Android, iOS, macOS, and Windows
- **App Distribution** - Enterprise App Store setup, build uploads, and beta testing
- **Integrations** - SSO configuration, CI/CD pipelines, and third-party tools
- **API Reference** - Complete API documentation with code examples

**To search documentation:**

1. Visit [learn.applivery.com](https://learn.applivery.com)
2. Use the **search bar** at the top of the page
3. Filter results by platform (Android, iOS, macOS, Windows)
4. Browse by category

All documentation includes step-by-step instructions, screenshots, and code examples where applicable.

## How do I get training and professional services?

Applivery offers training programs and professional services to accelerate your deployment and maximize platform value.

**Available training options:**

- **Video Tutorials** - Self-paced video guides covering common workflows and features
- **Live Webinars** - Monthly training sessions on specific topics (register through Dashboard)
- **On-Demand Training** - Recorded webinars available in the Help Center
- **Enterprise Training** - Custom on-site or virtual training for your IT team (Enterprise plans)

**Additional resources:**

- **Status Page** - [status.applivery.com](https://status.applivery.com) - Real-time platform status and incident updates
- **Changelog** - [applivery.com/blog/product-update](https://www.applivery.com/blog/product-update) - Latest feature releases and improvements
- **Community Forum** - Coming soon - Peer-to-peer knowledge sharing and discussions

**Best practices:**

- Bookmark [learn.applivery.com](https://learn.applivery.com) for quick access to documentation
- Subscribe to webinar notifications in your Workspace settings
- Check the Status Page before submitting tickets for platform-wide issues
- Review the changelog monthly to stay updated on new features
- Contact your CSM (Enterprise) for strategic guidance and best practices
