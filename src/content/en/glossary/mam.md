---
term: Mobile Application Management (MAM)
definition: A framework for controlling and securing mobile applications independently from the device itself, focusing on app-level policies rather than device-wide restrictions.
description: MAM enables granular control over individual applications, including data access, sharing permissions, and authentication requirements without full device management.
