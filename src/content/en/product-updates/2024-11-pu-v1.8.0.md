Greetings, Applivery users! We're excited to bring you our comprehensive November update packed with powerful new features and optimizations. This release focuses on giving you more control over device management, streamlining your app distribution workflow, and enhancing security across your mobile fleet. Let's dive in!

**Quick notes before we start:**

- Scheduled sync configuration available in Device Management > Settings > Apple Configuration section
- Manual commands and forced updates remain available even during scheduled offline windows
- Device inventory supports full lifecycle tracking from acquisition to decommissioning including updates, repairs, and relocations
- Build attachments streamline workflows by keeping all documentation readily available throughout the app distribution process
- **Important:** API domain migration requires configuration updates before January 31 deadline to avoid service disruption
