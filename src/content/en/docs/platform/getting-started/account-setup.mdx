# What is managing workspaces and how does it improve app and device governance?

Managing workspaces in Applivery provides an essential layer of governance over your apps and devices by centralizing billing and settings at the workspace level. This enables more intuitive management of subscriptions, ownership, and administrative controls across your organization.

## How can you switch between different workspaces and what options are available?

You can easily switch between workspaces by going to the top navigation bar and clicking the workspace dropdown menu that contains your current workspace name. Then select the workspace you want to go. Under the Workspace dropdown menu you will also find the following options:  
- People  
- Audit  
- Billing  
- Settings  

![workspace dropdown menu with billing and settings options](https://www.applivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Screenshot-2024-02-19-at-170950-1024x651.png)

### How do you create a new workspace in Applivery?

To create your workspace go to the top navigation bar and click the workspace dropdown menu that contains your current workspace name. Then click the + New workspace button and type a name.  

![new workspace creation interface in Applivery](https://www.applivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Screenshot-2024-02-19-at-171043-1024x651.png)

**Applivery TL;DR:**  
- Manage multiple apps and devices by managing workspaces with centralized billing  
- Switch workspaces quickly via the top navigation dropdown menu  
- Create new workspaces directly from the dropdown for organized governance  

## What billing and payment information is managed at the workspace level?

Billing and payment information is managed at the Workspace level in Applivery. Here you will be able to change between plans, add additional features, manage your subscriptions, and access your billing history. To do so, please:  

1. Make sure you have selected your Workspace from the navigation bar dropdown menu.  
2. Go to the Billing section.  
3. Use the left-side menu to navigate through the different sections:  
   - Plan & Usage: choose plans, upgrade, cancel, and see your current plan usage.  
   - Payment: manage billing information and payment methods.  
   - Invoices: history of invoices associated with your workspace.  

![billing section with plan and payment options](https://www.applivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Screenshot-2024-02-19-at-171318-1024x651.png)

## How do you manage your subscription plan inside a workspace?

You can upgrade or cancel your plan at any time. Just click the Manage link under the pricing of your current plan. If you want to choose a new plan to your account, use the Choose a plan button.  

![subscription management with upgrade and cancel options](https://www.applivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Screenshot-2024-02-20-at-004709-1024x651.png)  

A modal view will be prompted, displaying your current plan and the different alternatives you have to upgrade. Just select a new plan from the tabs on the top and then click Continue button. You can also cancel your current subscription by clicking the Cancel subscription link at the bottom.  

![subscription upgrade modal with plan alternatives](https://www.applivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Screenshot-2024-02-20-at-004938-1024x651.png)  

## How can ownership of a workspace be transferred to another user?

[Once in the Applivery Dashboard](https://dashboard.applivery.io/)(https://dashboard.applivery.io/), navigate to your Workspace (1) > Settings (2) > Danger zone (3). Click on the Transfer (4) button located next to the Transfer ownership option.  

![workspace transfer ownership button in settings danger zone](https://www.applivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Screenshot-2024-02-20-at-005132-1024x651.png)  

Choose a user from the existing list of organization members.  

![list of users available for workspace ownership transfer](https://www.applivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Screenshot-2024-02-19-at-173700-1024x651.png)  

The ownership transfer will occur automatically, requiring no additional steps. The previous owner will be reassigned to the Admin role.  

![ownership transfer confirmation](https://www.applivery.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/Screenshot-2024-02-19-at-173709-1024x651.png)
